ABOUT

Flappy Fish is a side side-scrolling game in which you have to dogde obstacles by using its flap and charge controls. Charge is a special ability for a player to finish level as fast as he can . We can play this game in PC , Computers and also on other devices like Mobiles and Tablets . Because it is developed with three types of controls which is Touch control , Keyboard controls & Mouse controls . And its controls are shown below :


KEYBOARD &MOUSE CONTROLS
MOVEMETS KEYBINDS
FLAP SPACE/ENTER/RIGHT/LEFT CLICK
CHARGE SHIFT/C


TOUCH CONTROLS
FLAP TAP ON SCREEN
CHARGE SWIPE RIGHT ON SCREEN
